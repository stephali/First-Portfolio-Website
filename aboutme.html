About Me

My name is Stephanie Ali and I am currently a freshman at Florida Gulf Coast University studying Software Engineering. My passions revolve around the worlds of business and technology.

I would spend hours researching and learning about the newer details and features just for the fun of it.Something about the mystery surrounding the way they worked had always sparked interest in me.
